## Why I Keep Coming Back to NYC for Food

I'm from New Jersey but I visit New York City multiple times a month. The food scene here is on a completely different level. From cheap street food to high-end restaurants, you can find almost any cuisine imaginable. I always plan my trips around food — whether I'm coming in on the PATH train or driving through the Lincoln Tunnel. This guide shares my personal favorite spots and experiences after many visits.

## My Personal Top 5 When I Visit

1. **Halal Guys** – Chicken over rice with extra white sauce (2am special). This is my go-to late night meal after hanging out in the city.
2. **Los Tacos No.1** – Adobada taco with pineapple. The best tacos I've had outside of Mexico.
3. **Katz's Delicatessen** – Pastrami on rye (messy but worth it). A true NYC classic since 1888.
4. **Xi'an Famous Foods** – Spicy cumin lamb noodles. Warning: very spicy but addictive.
5. **Levain Bakery** – Warm chocolate chip cookie. These are huge and best eaten fresh.

## Food Markets I Hit Up

I love visiting NYC food markets because they let you try many different things in one place. Here are my favorite ones:

| Market                | Vibe                          | When I Go              | Why I Recommend It |
|-----------------------|-------------------------------|------------------------|--------------------|
| Chelsea Market        | Busy & tourist-friendly       | Weekday mornings       | Huge variety of food stalls, great for quick bites and shopping |
| Smorgasburg           | Hip & lively outdoor market   | Sunday afternoons      | One of the biggest food markets in Brooklyn with creative vendors |
| Queens Night Market   | Super diverse & fun           | Saturday nights        | Incredible global street food from all over the world |
| Grand Central Market  | Upscale & convenient          | Lunch time             | High-quality food right inside Grand Central Station |

These markets are perfect whether you're visiting for a few hours or spending the whole day exploring. I usually combine them with my other food stops.

## Final Thoughts

New York City’s food scene is one of the main reasons I keep coming back from Jersey. Every visit feels like a food adventure. If you’re planning a trip, don’t just go to the tourist spots — try the local favorites and the markets. You won’t regret it!
