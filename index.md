## Why I Keep Coming Back to NYC for Food

I'm from New Jersey but I visit New York all the time because the food is just on another level. Whether I'm taking the PATH train or driving in, I always plan my trips around what I'm going to eat. This is my personal list of spots I hit when I'm in the city.

## Iconic Foods by Borough

| Borough        | What to Get                | Where I Go                     | Price | My Notes                          |
|----------------|----------------------------|--------------------------------|-------|-----------------------------------|
| Manhattan      | Pizza Slice                | Joe's Pizza                    | $     | Quick, cheap, and always hits     |
| Brooklyn       | Bagel + Lox                | Ess-a-Bagel                    | $$    | Worth the trip from Jersey        |
| Queens         | Xiao Long Bao              | Nan Xiang in Flushing          | $$    | Soup dumplings are dangerous      |
| Bronx          | Italian Hero               | Casa Della Mozzarella          | $     | Massive sandwiches                |
| Staten Island  | Grandma Pizza              | Denino's                       | $$    | Crispy and garlicky               |

## My Personal Top 5 When I Visit

1. **Halal Guys** – Chicken over rice with extra white sauce (2am special)
2. **Los Tacos No.1** – Adobada taco with pineapple
3. **Katz's Delicatessen** – Pastrami on rye (messy but worth it)
4. **Xi'an Famous Foods** – Spicy cumin lamb noodles
5. **Levain Bakery** – Warm chocolate chip cookie
